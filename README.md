📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the financial performance of an FMCG business using FP&A (Financial Planning & Analysis) concepts.
It compares Budget vs Actual performance, evaluates profitability, and identifies key business drivers.

The goal is to simulate a real-world financial analyst role by delivering insights and recommendations for decision-making.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze Revenue, Cost, and Profitability
Compare Actual vs Budget performance
Identify variance drivers
Evaluate regional and category performance
Provide data-driven business insights
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Excel (Data preparation & financial modeling)
Power BI (Dashboard & visualization)
DAX (Measures & KPIs)
📊 Key KPIs
Total Revenue
Total Cost
Total Profit
Profit Margin (%)
Budget Revenue
Revenue Variance
Revenue Variance (%)
📈 Dashboard Features
Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
Budget vs Actual Comparison
Profitability by Region & Category
Cost Structure Breakdown
Revenue Variance (Waterfall Analysis)
Interactive Filters (Year, Region, Category, Channel)
🔍 Key Insights
Revenue exceeded budget in select periods driven by strong regional performance
Logistics and marketing costs significantly impact profit margins
Certain categories generate high revenue but lower profitability
Regional performance varies, indicating operational inefficiencies
🎯 Recommendations
Optimize logistics costs in low-margin regions
Focus on high-profit product categories
Improve pricing and cost control strategies
Re-evaluate marketing spend for better ROI
